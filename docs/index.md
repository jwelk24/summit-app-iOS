# Summit

**Budget, net worth, and money insights — computed on your device, not in someone else's cloud.**

Summit is a personal finance app for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch that gives you the whole picture of your money without giving away your data.

## What Summit does

- **Budgeting that adapts** — monthly budgets with goals, rollover, spending pace, and one-tap "stay on track" funding for savings targets.
- **Net worth, complete** — bank, credit, investment, and manual accounts, with trends, projections, and portfolio allocation.
- **Safe to Spend** — a single number for what you can spend today before upcoming bills, on your Home Screen, Lock Screen, and Apple Watch.
- **Insights without surveillance** — a deterministic money coach, a 0–100 financial health score with history, anomaly alerts, subscription tracking, and refund tracking. All computed on-device.
- **Cash-flow forecasting** — see your balance weeks ahead, plan paychecks, and run what-if scenarios.
- **Reports that answer questions** — spending flow, category trends, tag filters, period comparisons, and tax-time exports.
- **Built for households** — share a budget in real time, split expenses, and settle up.
- **Challenges & check-ins** — money missions verified against your real transactions, a 3-minute weekly review, and your year in review.

## Privacy, by design

- All analysis and AI runs **on your device**. Transaction content is never sent to a server for analysis and never trains any model.
- Cloud sync stores **your data in your account** for your devices and household — never sold, never used for ads, no tracking, no third-party analytics.
- **Local-Only Mode** keeps everything on your device. Full JSON export and cloud erase are one tap away.
